About Me

I am a UX-focused software developer and architect specializing in JavaScript, HTML5, CSS3, C#, ASP.NET, and Node.js and I am currently working as a senior software engineer at Stine Seed.

Prior to starting at Stine Seed, I was working as a contract creative technologist on the user experience (UX) team at Nationwide Insurance from April to December 2016. I was responsible for building UI applications and prototypes for numerous internal projects and acting as the UX lead on some projects as well.

I moved to Des Moines, Iowa in July 2015 where I was the development supervisor for Two Rivers Marketing until February 2016. In this role, I was responsible for leading our internal development team, providing strategic and technical guidance to clients like Bobcat, John Deere, and Danfoss, managing projects with other vendors, as well as acting as a lead developer on internal projects.

Before moving to Iowa, I was a senior software engineer at ShopAtHome.com where I worked on developing customer facing browser extensions for Chrome and Firefox using HTML, JavaScript, C#, and ASP.NET Web API. Throughout my career in senior software engineer, systems architect, and technical lead roles, I have developed important mobile and Web projects for clients, including the Department of Energy’s Rocky Flats Environmental Technology Site, Visa, Rambus, National Oilwell Varco, and more. I had an an article on developing hybrid mobile applications published in MSDN Magazine and I also have presented at Denver Startup Week and Experiencing UX in Des Moines.

I have two wonderful sons, Ethan, 6, and Colin, 1, with my beautiful wife, Andrea. I enjoy bicycling, baseball, and playing Lego with the boys, and I love to travel whenever the opportunity presents itself.

Projects

What I Eat

A simple food journal for Windows Phone

Want to keep track of what you eat but don't want all of the calorie databases and granular measurements? What I Eat is the app for you! You control the granularity and timing of the entries, and you control what you see.

  • Export data to Excel via OneDrive for further analysis!
  • Color code and categorize entries by meal type.
  • Optional synchronization with Windows Azure Mobile Services in preparation for a Windows 8/RT companion
  • Full Windows Phone 8 Support
  • Machine translated for broad localization!

Stirling Birthday

Stirling Birthday displays your friends' upcoming birthdays on a beautiful live tile!

  • Call, Text, or Email birthday greetings from the app
  • Quickly see all birthdays upcoming in the next month, birthdays from the previous month, and all birthdays for all of your contacts
  • Share your birthday greetings on social networks
  • Full support for all Windows Phone tile sizes
  • Fully localized into 38 languages

Stirling Money

Stirling Money replaces your checkbook register and does all of the math for you.

Features:

  • Manage multiple accounts
  • Pin live tiles for direct access to an individual account
  • Plan and track budgets and financial goals
  • Visualize your financial health and progress with a series of charts
  • Search transactions to find individual transactions within an account
  • Tap and hold to edit accounts, transactions, categories, budgets, and goals
  • Backup to and restore from OneDrive
  • Synchronize your data between family members so you can always know what your financial status is
  • Machine translated into 37 languages
