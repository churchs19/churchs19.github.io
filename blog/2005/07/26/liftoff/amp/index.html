Liftoff!

Jul 26 2005

This morning at 8:39 AM Mountain Time, the Space Shuttle Discovery launched in the first launch since the Columbia disaster. It’s good to see the space shuttles flying again as the first step to continung space exploration. More coverage is on NASA’s Return to Flight page.
