Fully Feedly

Nov 3 2016

Over the past few months, I’ve been contributing to an open source Chrome extension, FullyFeedly on GitHub. It’s an extension I use every day to view the full content of my Feedly feeds inline instead of having to constantly open new tabs.

It’s been a pleasure to work with Andrea Grandi to improve this great extension. It’s allowed me to keep my Chrome extension knowledge up to date and to contribute back to the community at the same time.
